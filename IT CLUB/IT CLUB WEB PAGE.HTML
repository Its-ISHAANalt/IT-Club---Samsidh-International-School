    📜 About Us

    The IT Club at Samsidh International School, Electronic City is a vibrant community of young innovators, coders, designers, and tech enthusiasts. Our mission is to empower students with the skills and confidence to explore, experiment, and excel in the digital world.

    We create a fun and supportive environment where students collaborate on projects, participate in hands-on workshops, and explore the latest trends in technology. Whether it's designing websites, building apps, hosting quizzes, or learning about AI and robotics — the IT Club is the perfect place to turn curiosity into creation.
